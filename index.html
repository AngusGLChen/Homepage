About Me

I am serving as a Lecturer (a.k.a. Assistant Professor) at the Faculty of Information Technology at Monash University. Before joining Monash University, I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Delft University of Technology, under the supervision of Geert-Jan Houben and Claudia Hauff. My current research focus is to apply language technologies to build effective educational applications.
